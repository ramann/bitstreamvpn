Instructions

How do I install a client?

sudo apt-get install strongswan

How long do I have to wait for my payment to be confirmed?

Your subscription will be active after your payment has one confirmation on the Bitcoin network. Typically, this takes about 10 minutes, but may take longer.

How do I create a certificate signing request?

We'll give you some commands like 
sudo openssl genpkey -algorithm RSA -out /etc/ipsec.d/private/vpn_client_key.pem -pkeyopt rsa_keygen_bits:2048
                sudo openssl req -new -keyform pem -key /etc/ipsec.d/private/vpn_client_key.pem -subj '/C=US/O=test/CN=d2a2419d-e6d0-46f7-9f58-23c2c2f210d8'
. Run these in your terminal and give us the output to receive your certificate.

What about your certificate? I need that.

Download our CA certificate and put it in /etc/ipsec.d/cacerts/bitstreamvpn-ca.pem
Also, download our endpoint certificate and put it in /etc/ipsec.d/certs/bitstreamvpn-endpoint.pem

What do I use for my configuration file?

Create /etc/ipsec.conf with the below contents:
config setup
        charondebug="ike 2, cfg 2"

            conn bitstreamvpn
        rightsubnet=0.0.0.0/0
        keyexchange=ikev2
        auto=add
        right=
        leftsourceip=%modecfg
        rightcert=bitstreamvpn-endpoint.pem
        leftcert=vpn_client.pem

What about a kill switch? Everyone else has that.

We don't peddle in marketing terms for firewall rules. Use iptables.
